# Meta Ads Performance Analysis (Facebook & Instagram)

1. Project Overview
This project analyzes Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising performance to evaluate campaign effectiveness across the entire marketing funnel — from impressions to clicks to purchases.  
The objective is to generate data-driven insights and actionable recommendations to improve ROI, targeting efficiency, and budget allocation.

---

2. Business Objective
The business required a performance tracking solution to:
- Monitor ad reach, engagement, and conversions
- Compare Facebook vs Instagram campaign performance
- Identify high-performing audiences, ad formats, and time slots
- Optimize budget allocation and improve purchase conversion efficiency

---

3. KPIs & Metrics Used
- Impressions  
- Clicks  
- Engagements (Clicks + Shares + Comments)  
- CTR (Click-Through Rate)  
- Engagement Rate  
- Conversion Rate (Click → Purchase)  
- Purchase Rate (Impression → Purchase)  
- Total & Average Campaign Budget  

---

4. Key Insights
- Analyzed 216K impressions, 25.4K clicks, and 1.3K purchases, achieving a high CTR of 11.76% and engagement rate of 13.56%, indicating strong ad creatives and targeting.
- Identified low purchase funnel efficiency (0.61% purchase rate) despite healthy engagement, highlighting conversion drop-offs at the final stage.
- Discovered females aged 18–30 as the most engaged audience segment, with India & Brazil driving high engagement volume and Germany & UK showing higher conversion value potential.
- Found Video and Story ads outperforming Image and Carousel formats across engagement and conversion metrics.
- Observed peak engagement during afternoon and evening hours, indicating optimal ad scheduling windows.

---

5. Recommendations
- Improve landing pages and offers to reduce conversion drop-offs.
- Launch retargeting campaigns for high-intent users who engaged but did not purchase.
- Reallocate budget towards Video and Story ad formats for higher ROI.
- Focus targeting on females aged 18–30, with region-specific strategies for volume vs value markets.
- Schedule ads primarily during afternoon and evening time slots.

---

6. Data Model
The project follows a Star Schema design:
- Fact Table: 'ad_events' (event-level interactions)
- Dimension Tables: 'ads', 'campaigns', 'users'

This structure enables efficient joins, scalable KPI calculations, and high-performance analytics.

---

7. Tools & Technologies
- Power BI – Dashboarding & visualization  
- SQL – KPI calculation & data analysis  
- DAX – Calculated measures and metrics  
- Excel – Data validation and preprocessing
